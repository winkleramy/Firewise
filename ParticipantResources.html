When assessing your home's wildfire risk, consider these three categories of home ignition. Of these, 90% of homes are destroyed during a wildfire event due to ember ignition in Zone 0.

For those new to Zone 0 home hardening practices or even for those veteran mountain residents who want to check-in on their progress, we recommend these free assessments: - CALFIRE's home self-assessment and low-cost retrofit options at https://www.fire.ca.gov/home-hardening - Santa Cruz County Fire Safe Council Home Ignition Assessment, request at https://www.firesafesantacruz.org/HIZ

The state of California is planning to require some Zone 0 practices to be followed by law, with enforcement beginning around 2028. A public workshop was held in March 4, 2025 for feedback.
